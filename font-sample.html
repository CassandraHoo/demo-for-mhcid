Moscow Mule Elixir

Refreshing and energizing probiotic rich cocktail with real ginger!

 

INGREDIENTS

  • Small handful of mint, plus some more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon ginger juice
  • Half of one lime, juiced
  • Half of one small lemon, juiced
  • Half cup ginger kombucha
  • 2 shots vodka
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, thinly sliced
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • 1 cup ice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Add ice into two copper mugs. Set aside.
  2. In a large glass, muddle the mint until it is fragrant.
  3. Add the ginger juice, lime juice, lemon juice, kombucha, vodka, ginger, and sweetener (if using). Stir until combined.
  4. Half of one small lemon, juiced.
  5. Pour the elixir equally into the two mugs. Garnish with mint leaves.
