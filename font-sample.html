Moscow Mule Elixir

Refreshing and energizing probiotic rich cocktail with real ginger!

 

INGREDIENTS

  • Small handful of mint, plus some more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon ginger juice
  • ½ of one lime, juiced
  • ½ of one small lemon, juiced
  • ½ cup ginger kombucha (I love GT’s brand)
  • 2 shots vodka
